Manifesto

Truth & Love through Computers

We believe technology should be a mirror that reflects what’s real and a bridge that deepens human care. We build open tools that return agency to people — so communities can create, learn, and thrive together.

Why this matters

We entered a digital age that optimized for clicks, not care. Data became extractive, interfaces addictive, and algorithms opaque. This is our reset.

“We didn’t come here to be the product. We came to be the authors.”

3dvr.tech builds transparent, open systems that return choice, clarity, and community ownership to the people who use them.

Three Pillars

✦

Truth

Open source, verifiable data, and clear interfaces. We design for comprehension over manipulation and make honesty a UX feature.

♡

Love

Inclusive, sensory‑friendly computing that reduces overwhelm and amplifies human connection. Care is our core protocol.

⌘

Through Computers

Devices and software as instruments for learning, creativity, and shared ownership — not surveillance. Tools that serve the soul.

What this looks like in practice

Open Tools

  • Web‑native OS concepts (TommyOS) for humane, distraction‑minimal computing
  • Decentralized chat, notes, and website generator using open web standards
  • Open hardware explorations and repairable devices

Design for Humans

  • Dark‑mode‑first, high‑contrast without glare
  • Minimal motion by default; gentle, optional animations
  • Readable type, larger defaults, and focused attention flows

Community Ownership

  • Transparent roadmaps and public discussions
  • Subscription that doubles as support + participation
  • Education to help anyone build, freelance, and thrive

Truthful Tech

  • Open metrics over vanity numbers
  • Local‑first where possible; privacy by design
  • Clear consent and explainable systems
