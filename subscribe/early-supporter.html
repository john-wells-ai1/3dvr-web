Supporter FAQ

Can I pause or cancel?

Yes. You can cancel anytime via the Stripe receipt email you receive after checkout. You’re welcome back anytime.

Do I still get Free plan access?

Absolutely. Supporters get everything in the Free plan plus all the perks listed above.

How do shout-outs work?

After you join, we’ll send a quick form where you can choose how (or if) you’d like to be recognized. We respect privacy and only highlight what you approve.

Need an invoice?

No problem. Stripe automatically emails a receipt, and you can generate invoices through the customer portal linked in that email.
