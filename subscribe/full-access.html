Founder Plan FAQ

How does “unlimited support” work?

Drop requests in the shared workspace anytime. Quick fixes happen same-day when possible; larger features get estimated and scheduled with you.

Is there a contract?

No long-term commitment. You’re billed monthly through Stripe and can cancel whenever you like.

What if I need more than a month?

Stay on as long as you need. We’ll keep stacking work each month and can also scope fixed projects if something larger pops up.

Can my team join the calls?

Yes! Invite co-founders, PMs, or devs. We’ll document action items after each session so everyone stays aligned.
