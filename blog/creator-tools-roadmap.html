Creator Tools Roadmap for 2024

June 4, 2024 • Product Team • Roadmap

From collaborative editing to immersive analytics, the next wave of updates is focused on helping teams build, iterate, and measure faster.

The roadmap below is organized into three phases. We will share monthly progress updates and invite select creators into pre-release programs. All features are tightly integrated with the authentication flow you already use at portal.3dvr.tech.

Phase one: Collaborative editing

We are rolling out multi-author editing so you can co-build scenes with teammates in real time. Presence indicators show who is editing which asset, and shared cursors make it easy to follow along.

  • Shared editing sessions with role-based permissions.
  • Commenting on objects, with notifications routed through the portal inbox.
  • Automatic conflict resolution powered by CRDTs tailored for immersive assets.

Phase two: Streaming and deployment

Asset streaming is getting smarter with adaptive bitrate delivery and predictive loading. Deployments through the portal will now bundle performance budgets and network health checks.

  • Edge-aware asset streaming with configurable quality tiers.
  • One-click rollbacks from within the portal release manager.
  • Health dashboards that monitor concurrency, latency, and crash loops.

Phase three: Insights and monetization

Understanding how audiences engage with your experiences is just as important as building them. We are expanding analytics, segmentation, and monetization tooling.

  • Audience journeys with funnel breakdowns across quests, interactions, and purchases.
  • In-world offer testing integrated with the commerce APIs.
  • Revenue reporting that syncs directly into your portal workspace.

We will continue to share public milestones here on the blog and give portal users early access to new releases. If you want to influence the roadmap, apply to the feedback cohort inside the portal or leave a comment below.
