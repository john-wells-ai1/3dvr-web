FAQ

Do I need a wearable?

No. Everything works without hardware. Wearables can enhance insights but are optional.

Where is my data?

Stored locally in your browser or device. You choose if/when to sync via encrypted peers (Gun.js).

Is this a separate app?

It lives inside the 3dvr portal and becomes a foundation across all 3dvr products.

Can communities run their own?

Yes. We aim to provide self‑host guides and open modules you can remix.
